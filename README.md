## 📖 Overview

**Photo Editor AI** is a free online AI-powered photo editor — no software to install, no account to create, no credit card required. Simply upload a photo, describe what you want in plain English, and get professional-quality results in under 30 seconds.

Built on **Google Nanobanano 2** and **GPT Image 2**, the editor delivers pixel-level precision across a wide range of editing tasks — from background removal and object erasure to style transfer and virtual hairstyle try-ons.
